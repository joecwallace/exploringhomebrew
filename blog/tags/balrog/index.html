H2O + Grain + Hops + Yeast = Beer!

Nashville's first festival celebrating the art, craft, and TASTE of homebrewed beer.

The event will recruit the best beer homebrewers in the region to provide homebrew samples at the festival. Home brew enthusiasts in Nashville are invited to taste product from local brewers and vote on their favorites.

Proceeds will benefit EXPLORE! COMMUNITY SCHOOL in East Nashville.

If you are a homebrewer and want to show your stuff, please contact info@exploringhomebrew.com.

Exploring Homebrew is a 3rd party special event, organized by volunteers. We'll be drinking beer to benefit Explore! Community School on May 13. Interested? Enter your email below, and you could be first to buy tickets!
