H2O + Grain + Hops + Yeast = Beer!

Nashville's first festival celebrating the art, craft, and TASTE of homebrewed beer.

May 13, 2016 ◇ 6-10pm ◇ Pavilion East

Presented by

Home brew enthusiasts in Nashville are invited to taste samples from local brewers and vote on their favorites.

If you are a homebrewer and want to show your stuff, please contact info@exploringhomebrew.com.


Proceeds will benefit EXPLORE! COMMUNITY SCHOOL in East Nashville. Exploring Homebrew is a 3rd party special event, organized by volunteers.
