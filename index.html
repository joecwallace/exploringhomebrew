H2O + Grain + Hops + Yeast = Beer!

Nashville's first festival celebrating the art, craft, and TASTE of homebrewed beer.

May 12, 2017 ◇ 7-10pm ◇ American Legion Post 82 ◇ 21 & up!

Home brew enthusiasts in Nashville are invited to taste samples from local brewers and vote on their favorites. If you are a homebrewer and want to show your stuff, please contact info@exploringhomebrew.com.


Proceeds will benefit EXPLORE! COMMUNITY SCHOOL in East Nashville. Exploring Homebrew is a 3rd party special event, organized by volunteers.
